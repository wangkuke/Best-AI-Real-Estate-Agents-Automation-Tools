Frequently Asked Questions

Will AI agents replace real estate agents? +

No, AI agents are tools to augment real estate professionals, not replace them. They handle repetitive tasks like initial lead qualification, appointment scheduling, and follow-ups, allowing agents to focus on high-value activities like negotiations, client relationships, and complex problem-solving. The human touch remains essential for closing deals and building trust.

How much do AI voice agents cost in 2025? +

In 2025, AI voice agents typically range from $99 to $500 per month for small to medium-sized businesses. Pricing is usually based on:

  • Minutes of conversation per month
  • Number of phone numbers
  • Advanced features like multilingual support
  • CRM integration complexity

Enterprise solutions with custom development can cost $1,000+ per month but offer greater scalability and customization.

How can I ensure AI doesn't give incorrect pricing information? +

Implement these safeguards:

  1. Dynamic Data Integration: Connect AI directly to your MLS or pricing database for real-time information
  2. Knowledge Boundaries: Program AI to defer specific pricing questions to human agents
  3. Regular Updates: Schedule automatic data refreshes to ensure accuracy
  4. Human Oversight: Review AI conversations weekly to catch and correct any issues
Is AI calling legal for real estate lead generation? +

Yes, with important compliance considerations:

  • US (FCC Regulations): Requires prior express written consent for marketing calls to mobile phones
  • Do Not Call Lists: AI must scrub numbers against federal and state DNC registries
  • Caller ID: Must transmit accurate caller identification information
  • Recording Laws: Comply with state-by-state consent requirements for call recording
  • International: GDPR in Europe, CASL in Canada, and other local regulations apply

Reputable AI tools have compliance features built-in, but it's your responsibility to use them correctly.
